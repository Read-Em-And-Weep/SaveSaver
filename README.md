# Save Saver
Mod designed for those who have bricked their save file by cheating in resources and completing story incantations early. 

This mod will force the reset object to always be available in the Crossroads. If you have bricked your save, simply navigate to the fountain with Hades's face in the Crossroads and reminisce from it. This will completely reset the story, as though you had never won against Chronos and Typhon. This should rescue your save.

As long as you do not cheat the boss resources in and complete the story incantations at the expected time, your save should be saved.
